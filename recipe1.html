Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chunk, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 large carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 3 large potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • fresh parsley, chooped(for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season the beef cubes with salt and pepper.
  2. In large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Brown the beef in batches, ensuring all sides are seared, remove and set aside.
  4. In same pot, add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  5. Add minced garlic and tomato paste; cook for an additional minute.
  6. Pour in red wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Let the wine reduce by half, return seared beef to the pot.
  7. Add beef broth, dried thyme and bay leaves. Bring to boil.
  8. Reduce the heat to low, cover and let simmer for about 1 hour.
  9. Stir in carrots, potatoes and celery.
  10. Continue simmering for another 30-40 mins, until vegetables and beef are tender.
  11. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  12. Remove bay leaves, ladle stew into bowls, garnish with chopped parsely and serve warm.
