Chapati

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon oil(for softness)
  • teaspoon salt
  • extra flour for rolling
  • 1 tablespoon butter(for brushing)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Add warm water gradually while mixing with your hand or a spoon.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes soft and smooth.
  4. Coat with a little oil, cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into 6-8 equal balls.
  6. Lightly flour a clean surface and roll out each ball into a thin, round disc.
  7. Heat a cast iron pan over medium-high heat.
  8. Place one chapati on the hot pan and cook for about 30 seconds until bubbles start to form.
  9. Flip and cook the other side for 30-40 seconds, pressing gently.
  10. Flip once more and press lightly; the chapati should puff up.
  11. Remove from heat and brush with butter.
  12. Serve warm with beef stew.
  13. Store in a clean cloth to keep them soft and warm.
